Frequently Asked Questions

How is my pension payment calculated?

Your pension payment is calculated based on several factors, including your years of service, your final salary, and the scheme's accrual rate. This calculator provides an estimate based on the data you input.

Is this calculation a guaranteed amount?

No, this is an illustrative estimate only and is not a guarantee of the benefits you will receive. Your official pension statement will provide a more precise calculation. For a full breakdown, please check 'My documents'.

Can I take a lump sum as well as a regular payment?

Most pension schemes allow you to take a portion of your pension pot as a tax-free lump sum. Please use the 'Lump Sum Calculator' to see how this might affect your regular pension payments.
